The official Wegap widget plugin for WordPress. Add live chat, video calls, and AI knowledge assistant to your website in minutes.

== Description ==

**Wegap Widget** is the official integration for adding the full power of the Wegap platform to your WordPress site. This includes:

- Live chat and customer support
- Audio and video calls
- Smart AI assistant ("Wegap Scholar")
- Contact center capabilities

== Installation ==

1. Download the plugin ZIP from GitHub.
2. Go to *Plugins > Add New* in your WordPress dashboard.
3. Click **Upload Plugin** and select `wegap-widget.zip`.
4. After installation, click **Activate Plugin**.
5. Go to *Settings > Wegap Widget* and enter your **Workspace ID**.
6. Done! The widget is now live on your site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where can I find my Workspace ID? =
Your Workspace ID is available in your Wegap admin panel.

= Can I customize the widget? =
Yes. You can change the position, theme, and default language from the settings page.
